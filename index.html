Save and Get loans at your convenience

It has always been in peoples culture to save money for thr future due to anticipated needs. People have created saccos that have done this work and have aslo provide other services like loans and bill payments. Sentesave has been developeed to bring these sacco services into the digital world because everything is now going digital and modernized.

SenteSave is made to fit on your mobile phone such that you can easily carryout transactions whereever you are at any time and any palce. You can also acquire loans and pay bills using the SenteSave app.
